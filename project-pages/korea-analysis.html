South Korea COVID-19 Exploratory Data Analysis
This project utilizes Pandas, Numpy, Scipy, and Plotly libraries to analyze and clean COVID-19-related datasets specific to South Korea. The exploration covers diverse aspects such as case distribution, patient information, time trends, demographics, and government policies. Additionally, the analysis involves the creation of interactive visualizations using Plotly on multiple dataframes, each focusing on specific aspects of the pandemic in South Korea.
