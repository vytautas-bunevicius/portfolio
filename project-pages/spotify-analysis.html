Spotify Top 50 Tracks of 2020 Analysis
In this project, I explore the Spotify Top 50 Tracks of 2020 using the Numpy and Pandas tools in Python. The aim is to find interesting details about the most popular songs, artists, and music styles.
Summary / Findings
I started by cleaning up the data, removing columns we don’t need, like ‘Unnamed: 0’, and checking for any missing information. Looking through the data, I learned about different music styles, which artists are common, and features of songs like how danceable they are or how loud they sound.
I discovered that Pop is the favorite music style and looked at how often artists show up with their songs. I used statistics to spot unusual songs, find the longest and shortest ones, and see how varied the music styles, artists, and albums are. I noticed that some music styles are more common, while others, like Dreampop/Hip-Hop/R&B, don’t show up as much.
I also looked at how different features of songs, like how loud they are and how much energy they have, are related. To finish, I compared how danceable, loud, and acoustic the songs are in each music style, giving us a glimpse into the variety of music in each category. This whole study gives us a detailed look at the trends and patterns in Spotify’s most popular songs.
